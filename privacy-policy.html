Legal & Privacy

Your data.

Last updated: June 2026

Lockout London provides mobile auto locksmith services across London and the surrounding areas. This page explains what information we collect when you contact us, why we need it, and how it is handled. We do not sell your data, and we only use it to get your vehicle open as quickly as possible.
01

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect the following details to process your job:

Full Name
To address and identify you
Phone Number
To reach you about the job
Vehicle Details
Make, model, year
Location / Postcode
To dispatch to you
Service Request Details
Description of your lockout or key issue
02

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the purposes below. We do not use it for marketing and we do not sell it to anyone.

  • Responding to your locksmith service request
  • Arranging dispatch of a qualified locksmith to your location
  • Contacting you by phone or WhatsApp to confirm or update your booking
  • Following up on your enquiry if we missed each other
03

Data Sharing

We work with a small network of trusted, vetted locksmith professionals across London. Where a job is fulfilled by one of these partners, we share only the information they need to complete it — your name, phone number, location, and vehicle details.

We do not share your data with any third party for advertising, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to your service request.

04

Data Retention

We keep enquiry records for a limited period for operational purposes — for example, to follow up on open requests or handle any post-job queries. Once a job is complete and no longer relevant, records are deleted. We do not hold data longer than necessary.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

05

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. If you'd like to exercise these rights, please get in touch via WhatsApp and we'll respond promptly.

06

Privacy Questions

Get in touch with us directly

We aim to respond to all privacy queries within 48 hours.
